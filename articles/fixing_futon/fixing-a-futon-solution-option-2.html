Fixing a Futon - Solution Option 2
05 Nov 2006

Spare Parts. The best thing in the world if you have anything you don’t want to replace fully. Sometimes though, they are hard to come by. So many people would rather just replace the whole item.

I looked on the internet and had a hard time finding anything that even resembled the right part. I found one site that had something that looked right, but the price was too high to gamble on it.

Of course the next logical place to look was eBay. I found a seller that had a part that certainly looked right. I sent an email to the seller asking about the specific dimensions of the parts. I got referred to a website that had the item for a much higher price, but showed the dimensions. Looked this would work, but I wanted to check through all the options before I spent any money.
