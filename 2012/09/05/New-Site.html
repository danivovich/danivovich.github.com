Goodbye IvoWeb, Hello New
05 Sep 2012

This domain name has served me well for a long time, but it has never really represented me. It is simply an unclever combination of the first 3 letters of my last name, and web, the place where the domain lives.

The content on IvoWeb.net has changed a few times over the years, and the technology hosting as changed even more. Now that I have become less experimental with my personal site and simply use Jekyll, I feel it is a good time to relaunch the site, and focus on keeping the content up-to-date, rather than the software

To this end, I am launching a refresh of this website at danivovich.com

This site as it is will live on a short while to give the few RSS subsribers a chance to add the new feedburner url, as that seems to be the only thing I can’t seamlessly redirect. After that time, it will automatically redirect to the new site.

I hope you enjoy the new site.
