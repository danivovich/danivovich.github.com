RailsConf 2011 Twitter Summary
09 Jun 2011

Had a great time a couple weeks ago at RailsConf 2011. I didn’t post nearly as much to Twitter as I did last year, but for the sake of record keeping, here are my posts of the week.

  • Pumped up for #railsconf! Looking at a great day of sessions after rocking #bohconf and #igniterc yesterday. - 09:02 AM

  • Great talk from @j3 on presenters, dry, srp, and better Ruby code. Awesome session start for #railsconf - 11:27 AM

  • I believe PostgreSQL is awesome, but thanks to @ngauthier, I’m now sure it is. #railsconf - 12:11 PM

  • Great morning here at #railsconf, just set a high bar for the rest of the sessions I attend. - 12:25 PM

  • Wrapped up a great day of #railsconf sessions. Looking forward to this evening’s keynote and Ruby Heroes awards. - 05:09 PM

  • Really great #leanstartup keynote from @ericries at #railsconf. Great message and content, well delivered. Very much enjoyed - 07:38 PM

  • Awesome morning keynotes today at #railsconf. Thanks @tenderlove and @drnic - 10:26 AM

  • Thanks to @tmm1 for the really great talk about tracking down performance issues. Really great information and awesome tools. #railsconf - 02:46 PM

  • Awesome talk about using redis where sql databases can fall short from @jrirei at #railsconf - 02:47 PM

  • Great morning at #railsconf so far. Awesome opening keynotes from @abatalion, @coreyhaines and @danmelton. - 11:55 AM

  • Very interesting and informative presentation on api’s for the rich client with rails from @wycats at #railsconf - 11:56 AM

  • Amazingly awesome presentation on backbone.js by @mkelly12 at #railsconf. Great content, well presented, lots of laughs, even Ke$ha. - 03:04 PM

  • A great last day of #railsconf to end a great week. Really had an awesome time. - 05:38 PM
